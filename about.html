About COBSA-HYDERABAD CHAPTER

Congo Brazzaville Students Association (COBSA) has been created to represent and act on students’ views and concerns; promote, coordinate, and operate student’s activities and services; and enhance channels of communication between the students’ faculties, administration, alumni, universities, Republic Of Congo High Commission and other associations.
COBSA was started on 20th April 2014.
